What Are Drugs?

Drugs are substances that can change how your body or mind works. Some drugs are legal, like medicine or caffeine, but others are illegal, like marijuana, cocaine, and heroin. It's important to learn about drugs and understand the risks involved in using them.

How Drugs Affect Your Body

Drugs can affect your brain and body in many harmful ways. Here's how:

  • Brain Function: Drugs can change your brain's ability to think, learn, and make decisions.
  • Physical Health: Some drugs can damage your heart, lungs, and liver, and lead to long-term health problems.
  • Behavior: Drugs can make people act in dangerous or unpredictable ways.
  • Relationships: Drugs can harm friendships and family relationships because of the behavior changes they cause.
